About Us

Hey there! I'm Motheo Nisher

We are a small team of passionate crocheters who love creating unique handmade items. Our mission is to inspire creativity through quality yarns and patterns.
We are dedicated to making crocheting accessible to everyone as we see it as more than a hobby. Every loop is made with love. In terms of learning you get to acquire knowledge at your own pace from anywhere around the world. Join us and be able to craft your creativity today.
