Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I read a crochet pattern? Look for abbreviations like "sc" (single crochet), "dc" (double crochet), and follow row-by-row instructions.
  • I'm stuck on a step — what should I do? Post your question in our Facebook group or email us!
  • What if there's an error in the pattern? Check our corrections log below or let us know so we can update it.

Pattern Corrections
